Our History

Founded in 2075, New Frontier emerged from a coalition of scientists, engineers, and visionaries determined to find a new home for humanity. Over the decades, we have pioneered advanced space exploration technologies and built a dedicated team committed to this mission.

Our Mission

To explore, identify, and prepare habitable planets beyond our solar system, ensuring the survival and prosperity of future generations.

Milestones

  • 2075: New Frontier founded.
  • 2080: First deep space probe launched.
  • 2090: Discovery of first potentially habitable exoplanet.
  • 2100: Development of advanced terraforming technologies.
  • 2110: Launch of manned exploratory missions.
